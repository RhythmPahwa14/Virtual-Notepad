Virtual Notepad respects your privacy. This application is designed to work directly in your browser without collecting or storing personal data.

02. Camera Usage

The application uses your device camera only to detect hand gestures for drawing.

videocam

Browser Permission

Camera access is requested by your browser, and you are always in control and can revoke it at any time.

On-Device Processing

All hand tracking and gesture processing runs locally in your browser using WebAssembly. Nothing is uploaded.

No video or images are recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server, ever.

Camera data never leaves your device. Your visual input is processed in memory and immediately discarded.
